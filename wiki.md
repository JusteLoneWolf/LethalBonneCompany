<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JusteLoneWolf/LethalBonneCompany/main/IMG/Step1-2.png" alt="Step1-2.png" width="350"/>

**__Step 3__**: Search in ThunderStore the modpack "LethalBonneCompany" and install it
